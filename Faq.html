Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about credentialing, CAQH, and our process.

How long does credentialing usually take?
Typically 90-120 days, but Quad Solutions reduces the average to 30 days through proactive follow-ups and dedicated account managers.
Do you handle Medicare/Medicaid enrollment?
Yes, we manage all government and commercial payer enrollments, including PECOS and state Medicaid portals.
What is CAQH and why is it important?
CAQH ProView is a centralized credentialing database. We maintain your profile, ensure timely reattestation, and data consistency across payers.
Do you support small private practices?
Absolutely — from solo practitioners to multi-specialty groups, we customize solutions based on volume and payer mix.
Is your billing support integrated with EHR?
We work with all major EHRs (Epic, Cerner, Athena, NextGen) and can manage claims directly or via clearinghouses.
What compliance frameworks do you follow?
HIPAA, NCQA standards, and CMS guidelines. We provide mock audits and corrective action plans.
